What is Sardines?

Sardines is an app to see how busy public places are. Data is collected with an algorithm that uses bluetooth and geolocation data to calculate the number of smartphones in a given area. Most people carry a smartphone, so this becomes an accurate way of measuring population.
The app integrates Google Maps which gives users an interactive map that displays how busy places are. For the app to function properly, it needs seed data. This will allow users to get accurate and up-to-date information.
Private Beta Testers will look for bugs in the app as well as perform bluetooth scans around the Salt Lake area.

Request Access

How to install:

Install Sardines on Android


First you will need to enable 3rd party installs in your settings.
Have you ever installed an app outside of the Google Play Store?
If so, you can skip this part of the tutorial.

1. Swipe down from the top of your screen and tap on the Settings cog.


2. Scroll down and tap on ‘Biometrics and Security.’


3. Tap on ‘Install Unknown Apps.’


4. Tap on the application that you’ll use to download the files.
(most likely Firefox or Chrome)


5. Tap the toggle switch next to ‘Allow from this source.’




Once the APK is downloaded, click it and follow the instructions.
If it gives you a warning saying "Google cannot scan this file for malicious code" just click "ignore" or "continue anyway". Since the app has not been uploaded to the Google Play Store, their virus checker is not active.


Setup

Once the app has been downloaded you need to do a couple things before getting started. A Data Collector is a special role that gives you more control over the scans the app makes.

How to become a Data Collector:

1. Copy your User ID from the settings page.


2. Send an email to sardinesmap@pm.me
Paste your User ID into the body of the email.
To speed things up, include your name and the phrase “data collector” in the subject of your email.


Modes
Local mode: Allows you to collect scans on your device and later send them to the server. (This is useful if the central server is down or you run into a bug)
Live mode: Allows you to interact with the server (use the map and search feature as well as submit scans automatically).

Change Domain
During the beta, the server may change at any time. You will need to update the address when it does.

1. Go to the settings page and switch to live mode.


2. Enter the new domain (the current domain is 73.65.178.156)


3. Clear the app from your recents and reopen


Submit Local Scans

Local Scans are stored on your device and need to me added to the database.

1. Copy your Local Scans from the settings page.


2. Send an email to sardinesmap@pm.me with your local scans.
